# Photos

Drop 5–7 portrait photos here as `01.jpg`, `02.jpg`, … and list their paths in
`js/personal.js` under `PERSONAL.photos`.

When the user makes the **peace** gesture, the photos appear as floating
polaroids around the "I love you" text.

## Tips

- Portrait orientation works best — the polaroid frame is taller than wide.
- Long-side ~800–1200px is enough; bigger just slows initial load.
- Missing files are silently skipped.
